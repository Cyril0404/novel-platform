# Journey to the West

## Chapter 1: The Monkey King

The story of how the monk Tang Sanzang received the mandate from the emperor to go to the Western Paradise to obtain Buddhist sutras...

In the beginning, the universe was like a huge egg. Within it, the cosmic primordial混沌 was formed. After countless ages, the egg cracked open and divided into heavens and earth.

The radiance of the sun and moon began to shine forth, and the courses of the stars and constellations were fixed. The waters separated from the land, and all things began to flourish.

In this world, there was a mountain called the Mountain of Flowers and Fruits. It was situated in the Aolai Country of the eastern continent of Videha. On this mountain grew many exotic flowers and fruits...
