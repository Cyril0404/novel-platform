# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第8章

The highest good is like water. Water benefits all things without striving; it abides in the lowly places that people disdain, and so it is close to the Dao. Dwell in a good place, keep the mind deep and still, deal with others with kindness, speak with sincerity, govern with order, act with ability, and move at the right time. Only by not contending is one without blame.

原文：上善若水。水善利万物而不争，处众人之所恶，故几于道。居善地，心善渊，与善仁，言善信，政善治，事善能，动善时。夫唯不争，故无尤。
