# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第7章

Heaven and earth are eternal. The reason heaven and earth can be eternal is that they do not live for themselves, so they can live forever. Thus the sage puts himself after and yet ends up before, puts himself outside and yet his body endures. Is it not because he is without selfishness that he can achieve his own purpose?

原文：天长地久。天地所以能长且久者，以其不自生，故能长生。是以圣人后其身而身先，外其身而身存。非以其无私邪？故能成其私。
