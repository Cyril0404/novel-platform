# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第26章

Heaviness is the root of lightness, stillness the ruler of restlessness. Therefore the sage, traveling all day, never abandons his heavy load; though he may have honors and grand views, he remains calm and detached. How can a ruler of ten thousand chariots treat himself lightly in the world? Lightness loses the root, restlessness loses the ruler.

原文：重为轻根，静为躁君。是以圣人终日行不离辎重，虽有荣观，燕处超然。奈何万乘之主，而以身轻天下？轻则失本，躁则失君。
