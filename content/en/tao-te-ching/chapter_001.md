# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第一章
The Tao that can be spoken of is not the constant Tao.
The name that can be named is not the constant name.

The nameless is the origin of Heaven and Earth;
The named is the mother of all things.

Thus, always be free of desire, to observe its subtlety;
Always be filled with desire, to observe its manifestations.

These two come from the same source, but differ in name;
Both are called the mystery.
Mystery of mysteries, the gateway to all subtle wonders.

原文：道可道，非常道；名可名，非常名。无名天地之始，有名万物之母。故常无欲，以观其妙；常有欲，以观其徼。此两者同出而异名，同谓之玄，玄之又玄，众妙之门。
