# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第35章

Holding the great image, the world comes to you. Going without harming, you are at peace and safety. Music and fine food make the passing guest stop. The Tao that can be spoken of is bland and tasteless; the eye cannot see it enough, the ear cannot hear it enough; using it, it is inexhaustible.

原文：执大象，天下往。往而不害，安平泰。乐与饵，过客止。道之出口，淡乎其无味，视之不足见，听之不足闻，用之不足既。
