# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第24章

Those who stand on tiptoe cannot stand firm; those who take wide strides cannot walk; those who are self‑seen are not bright; those who claim to be right are not distinguished; those who boast have no merit; those who are proud do not endure. In the Dao it is called “excess food and superfluous conduct,” which even things despise, so the one who follows the Dao does not linger there.

原文：企者不立，跨者不行，自见者不明，自是者不彰，自伐者无功，自矜者不长。其在道也，曰余食赘行，物或恶之，故有道者不处。
