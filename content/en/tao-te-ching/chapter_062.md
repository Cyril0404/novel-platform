# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第62章

The Dao is the hidden depth of all things, the treasure of the good and the refuge of the not‑good. Fine words can be marketed, noble deeds can add to one’s standing. If a person is not good, why would they be cast away? Therefore, when a Son of Heaven is installed and three high officials are appointed, even if they present curved jade before four‑horse carriages, it is better to sit and advance this Dao. Why did the ancients prize this Dao? Is it not because seeking it brings reward and sin is thereby forgiven? Hence it is honored throughout the world.

原文：道者万物之奥，善人之宝，不善人之所保。美言可以市，尊行可以加人。人之不善，何弃之有？故立天子，置三公，虽有拱璧以先驷马，不如坐进此道。古之所以贵此道者何？不曰求以得，有罪以免邪？故为天下贵。
