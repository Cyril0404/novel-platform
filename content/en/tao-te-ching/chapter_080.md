# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第80章

Small country, few people; so that even though there are many implements they are not used; so that the people value life and do not travel far. Though there are boats and carriages, there is no occasion to ride them; though there are armor and weapons, there is no place to display them. Let the people return to using knotted cords for record‑keeping. They are content with their food, pleased with their clothing, at ease in their dwellings, and happy with their customs. Neighbouring states are within sight of one another, the sounds of chickens and dogs are heard, yet the people, until they grow old and die, have no dealings with one another.

原文：小国寡民，使有什伯之器而不用，使民重死而不远徙。虽有舟舆，无所乘之；虽有甲兵，无所陈之。使民复结绳而用之。甘其食，美其服，安其居，乐其俗。邻国相望，鸡犬之声相闻，民至老死不相往来。
