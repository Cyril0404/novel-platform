# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第54章

The good builder cannot be uprooted, the good holder cannot be released; descendants will keep the sacrifices without end. Cultivate it in the self, and virtue becomes genuine; cultivate it in the family, and virtue overflows; cultivate it in the village, and virtue grows; cultivate it in the state, and virtue becomes abundant; cultivate it in the world, and virtue spreads everywhere. Therefore, observe the self by the self, the family by the family, the village by the village, the state by the state, the world by the world. How do I know the world? By this.

原文：善建者不拔，善抱者不脱，子孙以祭祀不辍。修之于身，其德乃真；修之于家，其德乃余；修之于乡，其德乃长；修之于国，其德乃丰；修之于天下，其德乃普。故以身观身，以家观家，以乡观乡，以国观国，以天下观天下。吾何以知天下然哉？以此。
