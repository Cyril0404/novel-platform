# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第43章

The softest thing in the world gallops through the hardest; the non‑existent enters where there is no gap. By this I know the benefit of non‑action. Teaching without words, the benefit of non‑action—few in the world attain it.

原文：天下之至柔，驰骋天下之至坚。无有入无间，吾是以知无为之有益。不言之教，无为之益，天下希及之。
