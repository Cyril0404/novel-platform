# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第44章

Which is dearer, fame or the self? Which is greater, the self or wealth? Which is more injurious, gain or loss? Therefore excessive love must bring great waste, and hoarding much inevitably brings great loss. He who knows contentment will not be humiliated, he who knows when to stop will not be endangered; thus he can endure long.

原文：名与身孰亲？身与货孰多？得与亡孰病？是故甚爱必大费，多藏必厚亡。知足不辱，知止不殆，可以长久。
