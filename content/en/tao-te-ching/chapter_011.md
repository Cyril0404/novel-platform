# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第11章

Thirty spokes converge upon a single hub; because it is empty the wheel functions. Mix clay to make a vessel; because it is empty the vessel functions. Cut doors and windows to make a room; because it is empty the room functions. Thus what is present brings advantage, and what is absent brings use.

原文：三十辐共一毂，当其无，有车之用。埏埴以为器，当其无，有器之用。凿户牖以为室，当其无，有室之用。故有之以为利，无之以为用。
