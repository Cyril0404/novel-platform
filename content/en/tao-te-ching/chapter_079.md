# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第79章

When a great hatred is reconciled, there must inevitably remain some lingering resentment; how can that be called good? Thus the sage holds the left tally but does not press others for repayment. The virtuous keep the tally, the non‑virtuous keep the law. The way of heaven is without favorites; it always assists the good.

原文：和大怨，必有余怨，安可以为善？是以圣人执左契，而不责于人。有德司契，无德司彻。天道无亲，常与善人。
