# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第73章

Boldness in daring brings death; boldness in not daring brings life. These two may bring benefit or harm—who knows why Heaven hates what it hates? Thus even the sage finds it hard. The way of Heaven does not strive, yet it conquers; it does not speak, yet it responds; it does not summon, yet it comes of itself; it is at ease, yet it plans well. Heaven’s net is vast and loose, yet nothing slips through.

原文：勇于敢则杀，勇于不敢则活。此两者，或利或害。天之所恶，孰知其故？是以圣人犹难之。天之道，不争而善胜，不言而善应，不召而自来，繟然而善谋。天网恢恢，疏而不失。
