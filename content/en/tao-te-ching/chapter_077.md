# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第77章

The way of Heaven is like the drawing of a bow: it presses down what is high, lifts up what is low; it reduces what is in excess and makes up what is deficient. The way of Heaven takes from the surplus to fill the lack. The way of man is not so; it takes from the deficient to serve the excess. Who can have surplus to give to all beneath heaven? Only one who possesses the Tao. Therefore the sage acts without claiming the result, accomplishes without taking credit, and does not wish to display his merit.

原文：天之道，其犹张弓与？高者抑之，下者举之；有余者损之，不足者补之。天之道，损有余而补不足。人之道则不然，损不足以奉有余。孰能有余以奉天下？唯有道者。是以圣人为而不恃，功成而不处，其不欲见贤。
