# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第61章

A great nation is like the low reaches of a river, the meeting place of all under heaven, the female of all under heaven. The female always overcomes the male through stillness, and by being still she takes the lower place. Therefore, when a great nation humbles itself before a small nation, it will win the small nation; when a small nation humbles itself before a great nation, it will win the great nation. So some are won by humbling, while others are won over by humbling. The great nation only wishes to gather and nourish others; the small nation only wishes to be received and serve. When both get what they desire, the greater should remain below.

原文：大国者下流，天下之交，天下之牝。牝常以静胜牡，以静为下。故大国以下小国，则取小国；小国以下大国，则取大国。故或下以取，或下而取。大国不过欲兼畜人，小国不过欲入事人。夫两者各得其所欲，大者宜为下。
