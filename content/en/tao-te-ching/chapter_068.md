# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第68章

The good officer does not take up arms; the good fighter does not lose his temper; the good victor does not meet the enemy; the good employer places himself below. This is called the virtue of non‑contention, the use of others’ strength, the harmony with Heaven, the ultimate of antiquity.

原文：善为士者不武，善战者不怒，善胜敌者不与，善用人者为之下。是谓不争之德，是谓用人之力，是谓配天，古之极。
