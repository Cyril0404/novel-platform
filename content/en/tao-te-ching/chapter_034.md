# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第34章

The Great Way is vast and can be on the left or the right. All things rely on it for life and it does not refuse them; having accomplished its work, it does not claim ownership. It clothes and nourishes all things yet does not act as their master. Forever without desire, it may be called the small; all things return to it yet it does not rule them, and may be called the great. Because it never regards itself as great, it can achieve its greatness.

原文：大道泛兮，其可左右。万物恃之而生而不辞，功成不名有。衣养万物而不为主，常无欲，可名于小；万物归焉而不为主，可名为大。以其终不自为大，故能成其大。
