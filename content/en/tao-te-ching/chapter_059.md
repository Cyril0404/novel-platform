# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第59章

Governing people and serving Heaven, nothing is better than frugality. Only by being frugal can one be prepared early; early preparation is the accumulation of virtue. When virtue is deeply accumulated, nothing is beyond one's power; when nothing is beyond one's power, its limit is unknowable; when its limit is unknowable, one can possess a state. The foundation of the state can endure long. This is the deep root and firm trunk, the path of long life and lasting vision.

原文：治人事天莫若啬。夫唯啬，是谓早服；早服谓之重积德；重积德则无不克；无不克则莫知其极；莫知其极，可以有国；有国之母，可以长久。是谓深根固柢，长生久视之道。
