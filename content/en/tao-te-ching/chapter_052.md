# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第52章

The world has a beginning, which can be thought of as the mother of the world. Once you know the mother, you know the child; once you know the child, you return to guard the mother, and all your life you will be free from danger. Block the openings, shut the doors, and throughout your life you will not be weary; open the openings and attend to affairs, and throughout your life you will never be saved. Seeing the small is called clarity; holding to the yielding is called strength. Use its light, return to its brightness, and you will bring no misfortune upon yourself—this is called following the eternal.

原文：天下有始，以为天下母。既得其母，以知其子；既知其子，复守其母，没身不殆。塞其兑，闭其门，终身不勤；开其兑，济其事，终身不救。见小曰明，守柔曰强。用其光，复归其明，无遗身殃，是为袭常。
