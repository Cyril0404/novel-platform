# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第33章

Knowing others is wisdom, knowing yourself is enlightenment.
Conquering others requires strength, conquering yourself is true strength.
Knowing contentment brings wealth, perseverance brings will.
Those who do not lose their footing endure;
those who die yet do not perish are long-lived.

原文：知人者智，自知者明。胜人者有力，自胜者强。知足者富，强行者有志。不失其所者久，死而不亡者寿。
