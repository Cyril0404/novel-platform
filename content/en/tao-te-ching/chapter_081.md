# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第81章

Truthful words are not beautiful; beautiful words are not truthful. The good do not argue; those who argue are not good. The wise are not broad; the broad are not wise. The sage does not hoard; the more he does for others, the more he has; the more he gives to others, the more he gains. The Way of Heaven is to benefit and not to harm; the way of the sage is to act without contending.

原文：信言不美，美言不信。善者不辩，辩者不善。知者不博，博者不知。圣人不积，既以为人己愈有，既以与人己愈多。天之道，利而不害；圣人之道，为而不争。
