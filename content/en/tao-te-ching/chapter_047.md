# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第47章

Without stepping outside the door, one knows the world; without peering through the window, one sees the heavenly way. The farther one goes out, the less one knows. Thus the sage knows without traveling, names without seeing, and accomplishes without acting.

原文：不出户，知天下；不窥牖，见天道。其出弥远，其知弥少。是以圣人不行而知，不见而名，不为而成。
