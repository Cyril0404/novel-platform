# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第75章

The people are hungry because those above them impose excessive taxes, and so they hunger. The people are difficult to govern because those above them are overactive, and so they are difficult to govern. The people regard death lightly because they are overly attached to life, and so they regard death lightly. Only those who do not make life a pursuit are superior to those who cherish life.

原文：民之饥，以其上食税之多，是以饥。民之难治，以其上之有为，是以难治。民之轻死，以其求生之厚，是以轻死。夫唯无以生为者，是贤于贵生。
