# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第25章

There is a thing formed of chaos, existing before heaven and earth. It is silent and boundless, standing alone unchanging, moving in endless cycles without ceasing, and may be regarded as the mother of all beneath heaven. I do not know its name; I call it the Way and name it “the Great.” Great means to go, to go means far, far means to return. Thus the Way is great, heaven is great, earth is great, and the ruler is also great. Within the world there are four great ones, and the ruler holds one of them. People model themselves on the earth, the earth on heaven, heaven on the Way, and the Way follows its own nature.

原文：有物混成，先天地生。寂兮寥兮，独立不改，周行而不殆，可以为天下母。吾不知其名，字之曰道，强为之名曰大。大曰逝，逝曰远，远曰反。故道大，天大，地大，王亦大。域中有四大，而王居其一焉。人法地，地法天，天法道，道法自然。
