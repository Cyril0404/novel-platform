# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第17章

The highest ruler, the people only know that he exists; the next, they love and praise; the next, they fear; the next, they revile. When trust is insufficient, there is distrust. He is leisurely and values his words. When his work is done and his affairs are accomplished, the people all say, “It is natural.”

原文：太上，下知有之；其次，亲而誉之；其次，畏之；其次，侮之。信不足焉，有不信焉。悠兮其贵言。功成事遂，百姓皆谓我自然。
