# Tao Te Ching

## Chapter 3

Not honoring the worthy keeps people from disputing.
Not valuing goods keeps people from stealing.
Not displaying what is precious keeps people from being confused.

Therefore the sage governs:
Emptying minds and filling bellies,
Weakening ambitions and strengthening bones.

Always keeping people without knowledge and without desire,
And ensuring that the knowledgeable dare not act.

Act without contrivance, and nothing is left ungoverned.
