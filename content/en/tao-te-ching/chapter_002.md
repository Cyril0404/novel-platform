# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第2章

When all the world knows beauty as beauty, there is already ugliness. When all know goodness as goodness, there is already not-goodness. Therefore being and non-being give birth to each other, difficulty and ease complete each other, long and short contrast each other, high and low lean on each other, sound and voice harmonize each other, front and back follow each other. Thus the sage practices non-action and carries out teaching without words. The ten thousand things arise and he does not turn away from them. He gives them life but does not possess them, acts but does not claim credit, accomplishes but does not dwell in it. Only because he does not dwell in it can it never leave him.

原文：天下皆知美之为美，斯恶已；皆知善之为善，斯不善已。故有无相生，难易相成，长短相较，高下相倾，音声相和，前后相随。是以圣人处无为之事，行不言之教，万物作焉而不辞，生而不有，为而不恃，功成而弗居。夫唯弗居，是以不去。
