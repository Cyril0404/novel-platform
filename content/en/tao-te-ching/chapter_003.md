# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第3章

Not elevating the worthy makes the people not contend; not prizing rare goods makes the people not steal; not displaying desirable things keeps the people's hearts from being disordered. Thus the sage’s governance empties their hearts and fills their bellies, weakens their ambitions and strengthens their bones. Constantly keep the people without knowledge and without desire, so that the clever will not dare to act. Practice non‑action, and nothing will be left ungoverned.

原文：不尚贤，使民不争；不贵难得之货，使民不为盗；不见可欲，使民心不乱。是以圣人之治，虚其心，实其腹；弱其志，强其骨。常使民无知无欲，使夫智者不敢为也。为无为，则无不治。
