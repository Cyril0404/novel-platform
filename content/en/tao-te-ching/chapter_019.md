# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第19章

Banish the sage, discard wisdom, and the people will benefit a hundredfold.  
Banish benevolence, discard righteousness, and the people will return to filial piety and love.  
Banish cleverness, discard profit, and thieves and robbers will have no place.  
These three are not enough as empty words, so let them be attached to something: be plain, hold to simplicity, have few selfish desires.

原文：绝圣弃智，民利百倍；绝仁弃义，民复孝慈；绝巧弃利，盗贼无有。此三者以为文不足，故令有所属：见素抱朴，少私寡欲。
