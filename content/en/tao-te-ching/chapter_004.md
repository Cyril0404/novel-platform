# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第4章

The Dao is empty, yet its use is never exhausted; deep and vast, it seems the source of all things. It blunts its sharpness, unties its tangles, softens its brightness, and becomes one with its dust. Pure and still, it seems to exist; I do not know whose child it is; it appears before the Emperor.

原文：道冲而用之或不盈，渊兮似万物之宗。挫其锐，解其纷，和其光，同其尘。湛兮似或存，吾不知谁之子，象帝之先。
