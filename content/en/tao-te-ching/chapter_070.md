# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第70章

My teachings are extremely easy to understand, extremely easy to practice. Yet no one in the world can understand them, can practice them. Words have a source, actions have a master. Only because they lack knowledge do they not know me. Those who know me are few, and the one who follows me is esteemed. Therefore the sage wears coarse clothing but carries jade within.

原文：吾言甚易知，甚易行。天下莫能知，莫能行。言有宗，事有君。夫唯无知，是以不我知。知我者希，则我者贵。是以圣人被褐怀玉。
