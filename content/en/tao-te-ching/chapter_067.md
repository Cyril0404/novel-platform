# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第67章

All under heaven says my way is great, yet it seems unlike anything. Because it is great, it indeed seems unlike anything. If it were like something, it would have become small long ago! I have three treasures that I hold and preserve: the first is compassion, the second is frugality, the third is not daring to be ahead of the world. Compassion therefore can be brave, frugality therefore can be generous,

原文：天下皆谓我道大，似不肖。夫唯大，故似不肖。若肖，久矣其细也夫！我有三宝，持而保之：一曰慈，二曰俭，三曰不敢为天下先。慈故能勇，俭故能广，不敢为天下先故能成器长。今舍慈且勇，舍俭且广，舍后且先，死矣！夫慈，以战则胜，以守则固。天将救之，以慈卫之。
