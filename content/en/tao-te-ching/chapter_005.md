# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第5章

Heaven and earth are ruthless, treating the ten thousand things as straw dogs.
The sage is ruthless, treating the people as straw dogs.
Is not the space between heaven and earth like a bellows?
Empty, yet inexhaustible.
The more it moves, the more it yields.
Much speaking exhausts itself;
Better to hold to the center.

原文：天地不仁，以万物为刍狗；圣人不仁，以百姓为刍狗。天地之间，其犹橐龠乎？虚而不屈，动而愈出。多言数穷，不如守中。
