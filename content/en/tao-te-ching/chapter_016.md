# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第16章

Strive for the ultimate emptiness, guard perfect stillness. All things arise together, and I watch their return. The myriad beings, ever flourishing, each return to their root. To return to the root is called stillness; this is called returning to destiny. Returning to destiny is called constancy; to know constancy is called clarity. Not knowing constancy, one acts wildly and brings misfortune. Knowing constancy makes one inclusive; inclusiveness leads to impartiality; impartiality leads to rulership; rulership is as heaven; heaven is the Way; the Way endures; though the body perishes, it is not in danger.

原文：致虚极，守静笃。万物并作，吾以观复。夫物芸芸，各复归其根。归根曰静，是谓复命。复命曰常，知常曰明。不知常，妄作凶。知常容，容乃公，公乃王，王乃天，天乃道，道乃久，没身不殆。
