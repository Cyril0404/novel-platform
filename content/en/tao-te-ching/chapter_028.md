# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第28章

Know the male, guard the female; be the world’s ravine.  
As the world’s ravine, constant virtue does not depart; you return to the infant.  

Know the bright, keep the dark; be the world’s model.  
As the world’s model, constant virtue does not err; you return to the boundless.  

Know honor, guard disgrace; be the world’s valley.  
As the world’s valley, constant virtue is fulfilled; you return to the unhewn.  

When the unhewn is divided it becomes a vessel; the sage uses it and becomes the chief official, therefore the great system is not cut.

原文：知其雄，守其雌，为天下溪。为天下溪，常德不离，复归于婴儿。知其白，守其黑，为天下式。为天下式，常德不忒，复归于无极。知其荣，守其辱，为天下谷。为天下谷，常德乃足，复归于朴。朴散则为器，圣人用之，则为官长，故大制不割。
