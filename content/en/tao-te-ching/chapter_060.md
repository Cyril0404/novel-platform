# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第60章

Governing a large country is like cooking a small fish. When the Tao is present under heaven, its ghosts have no power. Not that the ghosts are powerless, but that their power does not harm people. Not that their power does not harm people, the sage also does not harm people. Since neither harms the other, virtue is restored to them.

原文：治大国若烹小鲜。以道莅天下，其鬼不神。非其鬼不神，其神不伤人；非其神不伤人，圣人亦不伤人。夫两不相伤，故德交归焉。
