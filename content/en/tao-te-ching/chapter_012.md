# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第12章

Five colors blind the eyes, five sounds deafen the ears, five flavors spoil the mouth. Racing and hunting make the heart frantic, rare goods cause one’s conduct to be improper. Therefore the sage provides for the belly rather than the eyes, and thus discards that and takes this.

原文：五色令人目盲，五音令人耳聋，五味令人口爽，驰骋畋猎令人心发狂，难得之货令人行妨。是以圣人为腹不为目，故去彼取此。
