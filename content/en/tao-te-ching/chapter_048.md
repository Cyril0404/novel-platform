# 道德经（Dao De Jing）
## 第48章

In learning, each day adds something; in the pursuit of the Dao, each day takes something away. Take away and take away again, until you reach non‑action; by non‑action nothing is left undone. To take the empire, keep to doing nothing; if you act, you are not fit to take the empire.

原文：为学日益，为道日损，损之又损，以至于无为，无为而无不为。取天下常以无事，及其有事，不足以取天下。
